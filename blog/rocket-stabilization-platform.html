Rocket Stabilization Platform

January 2023: A proof-of-concept rig was made to test a PID I was working on. It uses a 6-DOF Gyro to know its position and turns that data into movements for the servo. The nose cone and body were made in Fusion 360 and 3D printed, and the control surfaces were made out of cardboard to show the movement of the servos.
